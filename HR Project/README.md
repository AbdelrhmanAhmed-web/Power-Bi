## üìä Dashboard Key Features
  ![HR Dashboard Screenshot](/HR%20Project/HR_Project_Dashboard.png)   
### 1. Employee Overview (KPIs)
- **Total Employees:** Overall headcount including historical data.
- **Active Employees:** Real-time count of staff currently in the company.
- **Terminated Staff:** Total number of employees who have left (Attrition Count).

### 2. Demographic & Age Distribution
- **Active Age Analysis:** A specialized chart showing the age spread of **current employees only**, excluding those who have left.
- **Gender & Diversity:** Breakdown of the workforce to ensure balanced representation.

### 3. Attrition Insights
- **Attrition by Department:** Identifying which departments have the highest turnover.
- **Trend Analysis:** Monitoring how employee exits fluctuate over time to pinpoint seasonal or structural issues.
